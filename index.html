The Valor of Mercy: Summoning the Strength of Compassion

“And We have not sent you except as a mercy to the worlds.”

- Quran 21:107

Mercy?  What is Mercy? Mercy is to have compassion or forgiveness towards something or someone. Why Mercy? Because of the challenge to be merciful. Being merciful might seem extremely easy but once put it in persepctive it is much harder than what it would seem like. When asked a question about helping a oppressor, Prophet Muhammad SAW stated," By preventing him from oppressing, for that is how to help him." This qoute highlights a main problem that is happening in the world today- having enough compassion to help both those who are being oppressed and those who oppress others.

How Clements implements Mercy: 

On April 20th Clements MSA held a bake sale to help a orginization called Camp Kesem

The main way on how Clements MSA shows the theme of Mercy in this bake sale is by the main message that they are trying to implement. Camp Kesem main goals is to help and support children and parent's cancer. By making this bake sale it falls directly under the definition of mercy( to show compassion or forgiveness toward someone or something). This bake sale is spreading sympathetic pity and concern for the victims of cancer and are also spreading the word about Camp Kesem to everyone who buys their products. Also 75% of all money that is earned from the bake sale go to Camp Kesem(25% goes to the club.)
