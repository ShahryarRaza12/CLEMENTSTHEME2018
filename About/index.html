WE ARE THE CLEMENTS MSA TEAM OF 2018

Hello! We are the Clement's MSA Team of 2018! We are here to help represent the muslim students at Clements High School!

What is MIST? MIST was originally started by a teenage girl in Houston around 2002 now it is a event that is taken place in 15 regions in North America. MIST( Muslim Inter-Scholastic Tournament) is a event that bring high school students together to help develop leadership skills or even inspire creativity. Another factor of MIST is to also help the students gain a deeper understanding of Islam and what it means to be a Muslim.

Clement's Mist Team:In Clements High School our MSA has around 30 members.Our Mist team has around 20 members.When applying for Mist many of the boys decided to play Flag Football and Improv. On the other hand the girls decided to go from a range of doing the MIST bowl to making a Nasheed. Although Clements has been known to not having that many memebers for MIST, this year however the officers made sure to keep everyone on track and always stay motivated for MIST.
